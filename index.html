About Me

Hello world! My name is Cecily Sanders, feel free to give me a nickname as many have. I know my name isn't very common but, I would say neither am I. I’ve gotten my certification as a full stack developer but, I tend to advertise myself as a front end developer because I thoroughly enjoy working with javascript and making something function. I’m very comfortable working in databases and get excited to learn about new technologies. I am versed in working with Node.js and creating server routes. To find out more about my skill levels in depth feel free to check out some works below or to contact me personally and ask.

Currently I am based out of Philadelphia, PA and this is where I’ve come to know myself as a developer as well as the technologies I’ve used. I have an associate's degree in Computer Information Systems and I plan to continue my education to get a bachelor's degree in Computer Science.

I love working with new people to try and understand the ways they go about a problem. Oftentimes I find myself working in team environments and confident in the work I can give to them. I love being able to contribute to a team and bring in a spark of personality to those around me.
